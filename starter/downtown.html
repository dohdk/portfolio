Downtown Museum's

Harrisonburg Museum's

Come and visit our multiple entertaining and educating museums here at Harrsionburg. Learn about Harrisonburg's history or even take an adventure exploring outer space. The museums provides many history and culture. We also have a great hands on experience for kids as well. Create and partake crafting activities such as quilting or even jewelry design making. For our family-friendly museums, visit our Discovery Museum which has a play kitchen, farmers market stand, newsroom, and much more for children ages 8 and up. With so much varities and topics to choose from, come downtown and visit our multiple museums that can offer a learning or fun experience for all ages.
