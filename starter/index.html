Harrisonburg City

The city of Harrisonburg welcomes everyone with open arms with its amazing atmosphere of passionate local food restaurants and sight seeing. Visitors can dive right in with its amazing food, outdoor adventures, and various community events. The historic downtown district is one of the most popular parts of Harrisonburg, filling your experience here with over 25 restaurants, 45 shops, as well as many sorts of entertainment. The Harrisonburg farmers market is a an amazing location to stop by, receiving the national recognition. Finish your visit by enjoying the landscape of the neighbor Shenendoah Valley Landscape. James Madison University

James Madison University is a public university located next to the Shenandoah Valley. The university was founded in 1908 and has become of one the nation's most innovative university in the south by the U.S. News and World Report. With over 19,883 students James Madison University engages its students with its prestigous faculty members, by creating relationships with them and making sure teaching the students is their No. 1 priority. In 2019, about 98% of the students who graduated are employed within six months of graduation. To learn more about James Madison University, click down below.
